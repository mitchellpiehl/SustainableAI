AI Helping Resource Management

AI is significantly impacting resource management in a variety of ways. AI is helping to enhance resource allocations by making smart recommendations by analyzing data and making predictions, and by optimizing resouce utilization by analyzing what resources are being inefficiently used. AI can also automize a various number of tasks, and do certain tasks more accurately than before, such as scheduling, reporting and resource tracking which minimizes wastes in these areas. Another benefit of AI is by analyzing potential risks before they happen so there is less waste. (CIO Dive)
