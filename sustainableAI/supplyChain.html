AI helping the Supply Chain

The use of artificial intelligence in the supply chain drastically improves planning, production, and management in many different ways. The ability of AI to process imense supplies of data allows it to predict trends and changes in the market, and improve decision making and efficiency of operations. This helps it to reduce waste and lower costs within the supply chain of any market. AI can also help optimize routes within the supply chain, minimize shortages, and help automate tasks. This helps increase sustainability by optimizing truck loads and truck routes to lower their carbon footprint. (IBM)

There are many compaines already implementing and seeing the benefits of using AI in supply chain management. In addition to helping increase sustainability, AI in the supply chain improves customer experience by helping to track packages, increase reliability, and provide more information about processes.
