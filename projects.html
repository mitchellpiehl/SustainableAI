Solving Math Word Problems Using Estimation Verification

Research Project focused on improving large language models abilities to solve math word probelms. I discovered a novel method that improved large language models abilities to solve math problems with accuracy roughly 2 percent better than previous best in the world. This method uses a estimation verification and rectification technique to ensure accuracy of equations generated by AI language models. A 14-page research paper describing my work and results has been submitting for publication.
